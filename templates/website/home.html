Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vending machines do you offer?

At the moment we're primarily focused on snacks, drinks, and combo machines depending on the type of location and the amount of space available.

During our visit to your location, we'll speak about which type of machine and which size of machine will best fit your needs depending on the context of who's going to be buying and the amount of space to work with we have.

How often are the machines restocked?

Machines are stocked depending on the volume of the inventory. We have tracking software built so that we know exactly how many products are in each machine at any given time.

Additionally, we may come in times when re-stocking is not needed but if new drinks and/or snacks are requested, we'll be happy to swing by & switch them out.

What payment methods are accepted?

Our vending machines can accept many payment methods such as cash, coins, credit & debit cards swipes and/or taps, and mobile payments such as Apple Pay and Google Wallet.

Do you offer customized vending solutions?

Yes, we select our products to fit the context of where the machine is located. For example...

We work with fitness centers where we primarily stock products such as energy drinks, sports drinks, electrolyte-infused water, protein shakes, protein bars, and the likes.

Additionally, we work with offices where we primarily stock teas, coffees, carbonated beverages, energy drinks, and the likes. We also work with warehouses where the workers primarily want sodas and energy drinks.

In short, we customize our product selection to meet the demands of the people who will be using the machine.

How do you ensure the quality of the machines?

We select the very best machines in the market. Why? Because we want to ensure that we're delivering a premium service, in addition, great machines don't break as often.

This means the customers are happy, and it makes our lifes easier by not having to repair the thing every other week. From a business perspective, a high quality asset is able to produce for a far longer time than a non-quality asset.

What if a machine malfunctions?

We will always respond as fast as we humanly can. We want the machines running 24/7 or as close to that as possible, so if it ever comes up, we'll be at the location at moment's notice to resolve the issue.
