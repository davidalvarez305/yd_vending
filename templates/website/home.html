Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vending machines do you offer?

We offer a wide range of vending machines including snack machines, beverage machines, combo machines, and healthy vending machines to meet all your needs.

How often are the machines restocked?

Our machines are restocked on a regular schedule to ensure they are always full and ready to serve your needs. Frequency depends on the machine's location and demand.

What payment methods are accepted?

Our vending machines accept various payment methods including cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments such as Apple Pay and Google Wallet.

Do you offer customized vending solutions?

Yes, we offer tailored vending solutions to meet the specific needs of your business, including machine branding and customized product selections.

How do you ensure the quality of the products?

We partner with reputable suppliers and conduct regular quality checks to ensure that all products in our vending machines are fresh and of high quality.

What if a machine malfunctions?

We offer prompt maintenance services and have a dedicated support team to quickly address any malfunctions or issues with our vending machines.
